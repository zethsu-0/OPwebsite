STORY ARCS

East Blue Saga

  • Romance Dawn Arc

    Chapters: 7 (1-7)

    Episodes: 4 (1-4)

    Luffy begins his journey, gets his first bounty, and recruits Zoro.

  • Orange Town Arc

    Chapters: 14 (8-21)

    Episodes: 5 (4-8)

    Luffy defeats Buggy the Clown and recruits Nami as the crew's navigator.

  • Syrup Village Arc

    Chapters: 20 (22-41)

    Episodes: 10 (9-18)

    Usopp joins the crew after they defeat Captain Kuro.

  • Baratie Arc

    Chapters: 27 (69-95)

    Episodes: 14 (31-44)

    Nami leaves; Sanji joins; Dracule Mihawk is introduced and scars Zoro; Fish-Man Island is first mentioned.

  • Arlong Park Arc

    Chapters: 27 (69-95)

    Episodes: 14 (31-44)

    Luffy and his crew battle the Fishman Arlong to free Nami's village.

  • Loguetown Arc

    Chapters: 5 (96-100)

    Episodes: 7 (45, 48-53)

    Luffy encounters Smoker and learns more about Gol D. Roger.

Arabasta Saga

  • Reverse Mountain Arc

    Chapters: 5 (101-105)

    Episodes: 3 (61-63)

    The crew enters the Grand Line and meets Laboon, the whale.

  • Whisky Peak

    Chapters: 9 (106-114)

    Episodes: 4 (64-67)

    Nefertari Vivi and Karoo unofficially join; Crocodile is first mentioned; Ms. All Sunday is introduced.

  • Little Garden Arc

    Chapters: 15 (115-129)

    Episodes: 8 (70-77)

    Giants and Crocodile are introduced; Elbaf is first mentioned; Nami is bitten by a dangerous insect.

  • Drum Island Arc

    Chapters: 25 (130-154)

    Episodes: 14 (78-91)

    The crew visits Drum Island to find a doctor, and Tony Tony Chopper joins the crew.

  • Arabasta Arc

    Chapters: 63 (155-217)

    Episodes: 39 (92-130);

    The crew fights Crocodile and Baroque Works to save Princess Vivi's kingdom.

Sky Island Saga

  • Jaya Arc

    Chapters: 19 (218-236)

    Episodes: 9 (144-152)

    Nico Robin joins; The crew meets Bellamy and hears about Skypiea.

  • Skypiea Arc

    Chapters: 66 (237-302)

    Episodes: 43 (153-195)

    Luffy and the crew fight Enel, who aims to destroy Skypiea with his immense lightning powers.

Water 7 Saga

  • Long Ring Long Land Arc

    Chapters: 19 (303-321)

    Episodes: 15 (207-219)

    The Straw Hats decide to hire a shipwright; Kuzan is introduced; the other two Admirals and Hachinosu are first mentioned.

  • Water 7 Arc

    Chapters: 53 (322-374)

    Episodes: 37 (229-263)

    Luffy,s crew arrives at Water 7 for ship repairs, and the Going Merry is deemed unsalvageable. Usopp leaves the crew, and Robin is captured by CP9.

  • Enies Lobby Arc

    Chapters: 56 (375-430)

    Episodes: 46 (264-290, 293-302 and 304-312)

    The crew invades the judicial island Enies Lobby to rescue Robin from the government, resulting in a battle against CP9. Franky, the shipwright, joins the crew after the battle.

  • Post-Enies Lobby Arc

    Chapters: 11 (431-441)

    Episodes: 13 (313-325)

    The Thousand Sunny is obtained; Franky joins; Usopp re-joins; the Four Emperors and Revolutionary Army are introduced; Monkey D. Garp and Monkey D. Dragon are revealed to be Luffy's grandfather and father respectively.

Thriller Bark Saga

  • Thriller Bark Arc

    Chapters: 48 (442-489)

    Episodes: 45 (337-381)

    The crew fights Gecko Moria, and Brook, a living skeleton, joins the crew.

Summit War Saga

  • Sabaody Archipelago

    Chapters: 24 (490-513)

    Episodes: 21 (385-405)

    The crew faces powerful foes like the Pacifista and the Warlord Bartholomew Kuma, who separates the crew.

  • Amazon Lily Arc

    Chapters: 11 (514-524)

    Episodes: 14 (408-421)

    Luffy ends up on Amazon Lily, where he meets Boa Hancock, one of the Seven Warlords.

  • Impel Down Arc

    Chapters: 25 (525-549)

    Episodes: 27 (422-425 and 430-452)

    Emporio Ivankov and Jinbe are introduced; many of Luffy's prior foes escape from imprisonment; Devil Fruit Awakening is first hinted at.

  • Marineford Arc

    Chapters: 31 (550-580)

    Episodes: 33 (457-489)

    Luffy participates in the battle at Marineford to save Ace, leading to the tragic death of Ace and Whitebeard.

  • Post-War Arc

    Chapters: 17 (581-597)

    Episodes: 26 (490-491 and 493-516)

    Luffy mourns Ace's death and decides to train for two years with Rayleigh.

Fish-Man Island Saga

  • Return to Sabaody Arc

    Chapters: 5 (598-602)

    Episodes: 6 (517-522)

    The crew reunites at Sabaody after two years of training.

  • Fish-Man Island Arc

    Chapters: 51 (603-653)

    Episodes: 51 (523-541 and 543-574)

    Luffy defeats Hody Jones, liberating the Fish-Man Island from his tyranny, and the crew heads toward the New World.

Dressrosa Saga

  • Punk Hazard Arc

    Chapters: 46 (654-699)

    Episodes: 46 (579-589 and 591-625)

    The crew visits Punk Hazard, where they meet Kin'emon and face Caesar Clown.

Whole Cake Island Saga

  • Zou Arc

    Chapters: 23 (802-824)

    Episodes: 29 (751-779)

    The crew arrives at Zou and learns about the Mink Tribe, while Luffy and Law form an alliance with the samurai to defeat the Emperor Kaido.

  • Whole Cake Island Arc

    Chapters: 78 (825-902)

    Episodes: 95 (783-877)

    Luffy and part of his crew infiltrate Big Mom's territory to rescue Sanji, leading to a fierce battle with the Emperor.

  • Levely Arc

    Chapters: 6 (903-908)

    Episodes: 12 (878-889)

    Zeus is unofficially recruited; Sanji obtains a Raid Suit; Imu and Aramaki are introduced; Luffy is unofficially declared the fifth Emperor.

Wano Country Saga

  • Wano Country Arc

    Chapters: 149 (909-1057)

    Episodes: 191 (890-894, 897-906, 908-1028, 1031-1085)

    The Straw Hats, together with the samurai, minks, and pirates, launch an assault on Kaido and his forces in Wano, leading to one of the most intense battles of the series.

Final Saga

  • Egg Head Arc

    Chapters: 149 (909-1057)

    Episodes: 191 (890-894, 897-906, 908-1028, 1031-1085)

    The crew meets different versions of Dr. Vegapunk
